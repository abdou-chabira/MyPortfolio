DealScope AI - MVP

AI Deal Intelligence & Competitor Tracker for Sales Teams. Features Implemented (MVP):

  • Company URL ingestion endpoint
  • Website scraping (title/meta/description)
  • AI company summary generation
  • Competitor detection (5 competitors)
  • Sales insight generation (pain points, objections, budget tier, buying signals)
  • Buyer-readiness scoring
  • Daily buyer-readiness recalculation with daily_delta and weekly_delta
  • Multi-contact personalized outreach generation (CEO, VP Sales, RevOps; cold email + LinkedIn + follow-up)
